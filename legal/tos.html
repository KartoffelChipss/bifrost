2. Description of Service ​

Bifröst is a Discord bot that bridges channels between Discord and Fluxer servers and relays messages between linked channels.

By using Bifröst, you agree to:

  • Follow the Discord Terms of Service

4. Server Administrator Responsibility ​

Server administrators are responsible for configuring the bot and managing how it is used within their server.

Messages sent in bridged channels may be transmitted to other Discord or Fluxer servers through the bridge.

5. Message Relay Notice ​

Bifröst functions as a bridge between Discord and Fluxer servers.

Messages sent in channels configured with Bifröst may be automatically relayed to linked channels on other Discord or Fluxer servers.

Users should assume that messages sent in bridged channels may be visible to users outside of the original server.

Server administrators are responsible for clearly indicating which channels are bridged and informing their communities accordingly.
